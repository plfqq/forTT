This Privacy Policy describes how Import stickers from tt by plf ("we", "the bot") handles your data when you use our sticker conversion service via TikTok Business API.

The processing of media content happens in real-time. We do not use your messages for any purpose other than converting TikTok stickers into a format compatible with Telegram.

We do not store your media files or message history on our servers. Once the sticker is converted and sent to Telegram, the temporary data is immediately deleted.

Data is shared only with the Telegram Bot API to fulfill your request. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.
